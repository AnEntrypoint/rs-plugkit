Wasm host-import pairing rule: any NEW env host import added in rs-plugkit (extern block use makes it live; LLVM dead-strips unused externs) must have its wrapper-side export landed in gm's plugkit-wasm-wrapper.js BEFORE or WITH the rs-plugkit push. A wasm shipping an import no published wrapper provides fails WebAssembly.Instance with LinkError and bricks every fleet watcher (witnessed: 0.1.809 host_fs_remove). Pre-push check: diff WebAssembly.Module.imports(new build) against the published plugkit.wasm and require NONE-ADDED, or coordinate the wrapper export first.
